Explore Exoplanet Data

Now try some customization! Use the dropdowns below to customize your visualization. The first controls the x-axis and the second controls the y-axis. Hover over any one point to display the exact values of both the x and y data.

Hint: for a cool visualization, try selecting 'Semimajor axis (AU)' on one axis and 'Orbital Period (days)' on the other. The resulting scatter plot exemplifies a key law of astronomy, Kepler's Third Law! This law states that the cube of a planet's semimajor axis is proportional to the square of its orbital period .
